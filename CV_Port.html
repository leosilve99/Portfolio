Recent Projects

M.I.O

Move In Office

This project was created in collaboration with a working group on the topic of "co-working spaces". Our research focused on the problematic issue of co-working spaces, namely the organisation of personal desk space. We thus created M.I.O., a totally innovative organisation system, thanks to the tray that allows people to move around the space without any problems.

Pock It

The smart reminder

This idea takes shape and inspiration from the famous Post-It notes produced by 3M. The need we had was to create a reminder for older people to take their pills without having to have those big plastic boxes. So we created a product that is easy to put anywhere in the house, in particular places that serve as a reminder (fridge, toaster, bedside table, etc.).

miXie

The stirring lid

miXie was born from the idea of creating a product that could help the user in the preparation of liquid compounds inside the pot. There are currently pot mixers on the market, but none of them allow the use of the lid. So I thought of creating a product that would cover the market gap in this field.
